The Win~Dow

The Win-Dow is a destination for classic smash-burgers, fried chicken sandwiches and dipped ice cream.
We are open 7 days a week and are available for pick-up and delivery.
Pull up a stool outside and hang out with our neighbors as you enjor our signature dishes!

We have three locations to choose from:
Rose Ave | Silver Lake | Venice Boardwalk



Double Cheeseburger $6.50

"WinDow is an excellent addition to a growing landscape of quality smash burgers on the west side.
Their burgers have the grilled onions incorporated in the patty, which gives them an patty-melt taste.
They also have a great salad option, crispy fries and delicious creamy milkshakes.

"Will definitely be back."
-Denis S.
